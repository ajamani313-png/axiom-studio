Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2025

1. Who We Are

Axiom Studio ("we", "us", "our") is a web and software agency operated by Abduljawad Amani, based in Rhauderfehn, Lower Saxony, Germany. We build digital tools and services for individuals and small businesses, including ReelDrop — a content scheduling and upload tool for social media platforms.

Contact: hello@axiomstudio.de

2. What Data We Collect

ReelDrop is a browser extension that runs entirely on your local device. We are committed to minimal data collection. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal data through the ReelDrop Chrome extension.
  • We do not store your videos on any server. Videos are processed locally on your machine and uploaded directly from your browser to the target platform (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube).
  • We do not store your social media credentials. Authentication is handled by each platform's own login system.
  • We do not track your usage or send analytics data to any server.

If you contact us via email, we may retain your email address and message content solely to respond to your inquiry.

3. TikTok API Integration

When using the TikTok Content Posting API integration within ReelDrop:

  • You authorize our application to post content to your TikTok account on your behalf.
  • We request only the minimum permissions necessary: video.upload and video.publish.
  • We do not access, read, or store any data from your TikTok account beyond what is necessary to complete the upload.
  • OAuth tokens are stored locally in your browser's extension storage and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • You can revoke our app's access at any time via your TikTok account settings.

4. How We Use Data

Any data we do handle is used solely for:

  • Performing the upload actions you explicitly request
  • Responding to support inquiries you initiate

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

All processing happens locally on your device. No video files, captions, or scheduling data are stored on external servers. Local extension data (queue, settings) is stored in Chrome's extension storage on your own machine.

6. Third-Party Platforms

When you upload content to TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube through ReelDrop, you are subject to those platforms' own privacy policies and terms of service. We are not responsible for how those platforms handle your data after upload.

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram / Meta Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights (DSGVO / GDPR)

As we are based in Germany, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR / DSGVO). You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority

Since we collect virtually no personal data, most of these rights will be satisfied simply by confirming we hold nothing about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@axiomstudio.de.

8. Cookies

Our website (axiomstudio.de) does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We may use essential cookies required for website functionality only.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy
